Fruits

Half your Plate is a healthy living initiative developed to
educate and empower Canadians of all ages to eat more fruits
and veggies to improve their health while providing simple and
practical ways to add a variety of produce to every meal and
snack. Half Your Plate is fun, practical and informative while
providing tips on how to prepare furits for meal and snacks at
home, at school, at work, eating out or on the go.

Computers

PCs and laptops are like extension of human lives these days,
especially if you're living and working in Ho Chi Minh City.
There are many kinds of computers and laptops suitable for
different needs. You can be a gamer who's building his own
computer, an expat buried in work or a student who just needs a
basic unit. Luckily, Saigon is up to date when it comes to
technology and communication so if ever you're in the need to
buy a new laptop are get repairs done, you can visit these laptop
and PC stores.
